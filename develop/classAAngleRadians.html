Detailed Description#

Initialization

Recommended way is to use operator literal format:

auto angle = 180_deg;
angle.radians() --> 3.14
angle.degrees() --> 180
auto angle = 3.14_rad;
angle.radians() --> 3.14
angle.degrees() --> 180
