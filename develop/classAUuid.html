◆ AUuid()

AUuid::AUuid ( const AString & s)

Construct UUID value from string. Supported the following formats:

  • Canonical: 8-4-4-4-12 (123e4567-e89b-12d3-a456-426655440000)
  • Raw: 32 (123e4567e89b12d3a456426655440000)
